Informed Consent

This portal is part of the Feed4Food project, which is about empowering vulnerable communities through our innovative Urban Agriculture Living Labs.

We hope to learn more about good and bad practices when establishing and running urban agriculture living labs. The information obtained from this study may benefit others who want to establish similar urban agriculture living labs in the future.

Your input will be stored in an anonymous way.

Taking part in this study is voluntary. You can withdraw your consent at any time. For doing so, contact the main researcher and indicate you want to withdraw your consent. The contact information, as well as a copy of this consent information, are available in the data portal.
